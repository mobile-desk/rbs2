Get help with eQ

Our eQ support centre has FAQs and troubleshooting guides. Alternatively, if you have a technical query (like I'm a system administrator and I'm having trouble logging in) you can contact the eQ Helpdesk:

UK and Channel Islands: 08458 007 274
Gibraltar: 00 350 200 47781
Overseas: +44  (0)1534 751 900

Lines are open Monday to Friday 8am - 5pm (excluding bank holidays). 

Calls may be recorded.
