A long track record of consistency and expertise

Our expertise in this sector is reflected in our consistent track record that’s thrived for over 20 years. This is the result of our ability to provide end-to-end banking, financing, and depositary solutions to asset managers and investment trusts. It is through our tailored and flexible financing solutions that a range of borrower needs are met, whether this is providing accordions, auto-extensions, Forward Starts Facilities or Floating or Fixed Rate facilities.

An integral part of this service also includes our speed of execution in order to put new debt facilities in place, and our additional support for asset managers with their wider banking needs through our diverse banking network.
